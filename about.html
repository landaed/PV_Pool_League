History

The PV Pool League started out as Panther Pool League, originally owned and operated by Inter Provincial Amusements. Founded in the mid-90s, it grew to encompass about 50 teams, experiencing fluctuations over the years. In 2007, WG Entertainment, owner of Inter Provincial, was purchased by Pacific Vending. In the following years, the league grew from 14 teams to over a hundred. In 2019, Donna joined to assist with league operations. By late 2023, Pacific Vending transitioned to new ownership under Landa Investments, led by Allan Landa.

Looking Forward to the Future

With the change in ownership and renewed energy, we are excited about the future of the PV Pool League.

About Us

Allan Landa acquired Pacific Vending on November 1, 2023. Our primary goal is to grow the league while ensuring it remains affordable for players. The launch of this new website aims to enhance communication and interaction with our players.

Pacific Vending is a proud member of several prestigious associations, including the Valley National Eightball Association (VNEA), Western Canadian Valley Eightball Association (WCVNEA), Billiards Congress of America (BCA), and Canadian Cue Sports (CCS).

Besides managing the league, Pacific Vending also specializes in home repair services for games and pool tables. For more details, please visit our main website.

League Coordinators

Our current league coordinators are Mark and Donna. They oversee scheduling, divisions, stats, and player support for the PV Pool League.
